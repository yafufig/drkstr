DRKSTR MEXICO

The urban fulfillment network for faster ecommerce delivery

DRKSTR is an urban fulfillment network for ecommerce in Mexico. It helps brands place top-SKU closer to customers through partner-operated nodes, enabling faster delivery without opening their own warehouses.

For brands For nodes

Urban fulfillment, not just courier delivery

A DRKSTR node works like a dark store or micro-fulfillment node: a partner-operated space used to store, pick, pack and hand off online orders.

The network is designed for local inventory, express delivery enablement, asset-light fulfillment, distributed fulfillment and partner-operated logistics nodes in Mexico.
